Web Design Best Practices

5 Practices to Follow:

  • Use clear and intuitive navigation
  • Ensure the site is mobile-friendly
  • Optimize images for fast loading
  • Provide descriptive alt text for images
  • Keep content fresh and relevant

5 Practices to Avoid:

  • Avoid excessive advertisements
  • Don't use autoplay audio or video
  • Steer clear of overly complex layouts
  • Avoid small font sizes that are hard to read
  • Don't overload pages with too much text
